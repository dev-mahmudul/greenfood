Welcome To Green Food

Green Foods and beverage is one of the rising food producing and marketing company Bangladesh. It's born in 2014 and we produces several types of foods and beverages and exports abroad market under the brand name of Green Food. Green Foods Co. is committed to conduct the business in such a way that demonstrates the highest ethical standards. The company believes that integrity is the imperative utility to succeed. Green Foods Co. is serving the best for the consumers to uphold the superiority in the market. It also believes that success depends on customers.


This is the primary criteria to fulfillment of customer needs. Green Foods Co. takes a comprehensive approach to all kinds of agro processed food products, considering all of the ways their lives can be enriched through ensuring hygienic and quality food products. Skilled and experienced personnel select finished products which are then examined in a laboratory to verify their quality and to check for residual substances both before and after the production process.
