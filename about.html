Welcome to Green Food

Green Foods and beverage is one of the rising food producing and marketing company Bangladesh. It's born in 2014 and we produces several types of foods and beverages and exports abroad market under the brand name of Green Food.

Green Foods Co. is committed to conduct the business in such a way that demonstrates the highest ethical standards. The company believes that integrity is the imperative utility to succeed. Green Foods Co. is serving the best for the consumers to uphold the superiority in the market.
